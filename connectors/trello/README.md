# @plotday/connector-trello

This connector syncs your Trello boards and cards into Plot, giving you a unified view of all your work across tools. It supports bidirectional sync: cards appear in Plot with their current status (list), assignees, and comments, and changes you make in Plot — checking off a card, adding a comment — are written back to Trello automatically. When a card moves between lists or is archived in Trello, Plot reflects the change on the next sync cycle.
